
# Product Vision

**Project:** Career Journal

**Version:** 1.0

**Last Updated:** 2026-07-01

**Status:** Draft

## Vision 

Career Journal helps job seekers manage their applications, understand their job search performance, and make data-driven decisions to improve their chances of finding the right job.

Instead of relying on spreadsheets, notebooks, or multiple job boards, users have one place where they can organize every application, monitor their progress, and learn from their results.

Our long-term vision is to build the most useful career companion for job seekers by combining personal application tracking, community-driven company information, and intelligent career insights.

---

# Problem Statement

Job searching is often unstructured.

People apply to dozens of positions across multiple websites and quickly lose track of:

* where they applied,
* when they applied,
* who contacted them,
* what happened during interviews,
* which strategies are actually working.

As a result, improving the job search process becomes difficult because there is little visibility into personal performance.

---

# Solution

Career Journal provides a simple but powerful workspace where users can:

* track every job application,
* manage their application pipeline,
* store notes and documents,
* schedule follow-up tasks,
* analyze their job search performance.

In addition, the application includes a community-maintained company directory, allowing users to discover and share company career pages.

---

# Target Audience

Primary users:

* Students
* Recent graduates
* Career changers
* IT professionals
* Experienced professionals actively looking for a new opportunity

Secondary users:

* Career coaches
* Mentors

---

# Product Principles

The application should always be:

* Simple to use
* Fast and intuitive
* Privacy-first
* Data-driven
* Community-powered
* Ready for future AI features

---

# Long-Term Vision

The MVP focuses on helping users organize and analyze their own job search.

Future versions will introduce:

* Automatic job aggregation from company career pages
* AI-powered job recommendations
* Anonymous community statistics

Career Journal aims to become the central workspace for managing a professional job search.
